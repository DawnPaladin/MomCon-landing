All Aboard the Animal Train features First Virtues, created by Dr. Mary Manz Simon (a MOPS board member) to teach important character traits to her children. The twelve virtues taught in this delightful book were chosen by asking MOPS parents "Which are the most important core beliefs for your child to learn?"

Character traits are somewhat abstract, and young children are usually concrete thinkers, so imparting these important lessons can be difficult. First Virtues starts each story with a simple definition of each virtue and then follows that up with clear examples from a child's life. Charming illustrations combine with concise, entertaining prose and practical advice to create a can't-miss parenting tool.
